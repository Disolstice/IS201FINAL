Why Start as a College Student?

Starting your entrepreneurial journey in college is the perfect time to experiment with ideas, connect with mentors, and leverage university resources.

Steps to Get Started

  1. Research Your Passion
    • Identify a problem worth solving
    • Understand your audience
    • Analyze the competition
  2. Build a Network
    • Join entrepreneurial clubs
    • Attend startup events
    • Connect with professors and alumni
  3. Launch and Iterate
